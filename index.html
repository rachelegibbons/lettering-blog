About the Author

Rachel Gibbons is a designer, developer, calligrapher, and lettering artist living in Portland, Oregon. By day she works as a designer at Metal Toad, a software enginnering firm, ranked one of Oregon's 100 Best Places to work, and by night she's a student at Epicodus, studying programming. In her free time she studies calligraphy and works on freelance design projects.
